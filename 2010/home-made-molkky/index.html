As I mentioned in my article about Akademy, I discovered a fun Finnish game named Mölkky and decided to build a set for the family. Following instructions from this page (fr), I bought a wooden garden stake and built a game of Mölkky with Clara today, here it is, note the "beautiful" typography :):

She was quite proud to help me saw the wooden stake. We played our first game this afternoon. It was really nice to see she enjoyed it, I only had to bent the rules a bit by allowing her to throw the Mölkky from a closer place and to retry her throw once when she missed. All in all good fun for a cheap investment :)
